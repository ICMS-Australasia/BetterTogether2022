Two Weeks to Go

There isn’t much time left to register for the Better Together 2022 Virtual Conference. With an action packed program of must see keynote speakers, engaging workshops and entertaining virtual social sideshows, what are you waiting for?

All delegates are entitled to:

  • Access to the online Conference platform

  • Access to on-demand content for 3 months after the Conference

  • Access to the online Exhibition
  • Access to all posters through the online gallery

Welcoming people with lived experience

The Better Together 2022 Conference organisers welcomes people with lived experience of disability, as well as those who support them. To help make our program more accessible for all, we have encouraged all our presenters to produce accessible content, all of our keynote sessions will include subtitles, and pre- conference access will be available for select content.

If you know anyone who might be interested in attending this educational event, please forward this email and let them know about the lived experience discounted rate.

Join the Buddy Program

For those of you with lived experience, who may be new to the world of conferences, we are offering an opportunity to virtually link with each other and members of AusACPDM, prior to the conference, to get a head start on establishing contacts. We also plan to offer opportunity during the conference to catch up.

Last Minute Reminders

As the start of Better Together 2022 grows closer, we wanted to give you some last minute reminders to ensure you get the most out of the virtual platform.

Pre-Conference Workshops

All the pre-conference workshops will be held on Tuesday 1 March and are only available to those who have pre-purchased a ticket to a workshop. By purchasing a ticket to a short or long workshop, you will have access to join live, view the content and watch the on-demand recording post conference.

Welcome Receptions

As this is a virtual event, we encourage everyone to participate in the social networking activities to interact with other delegates. There are two welcome receptions to suit delegates from all time zones, so make sure you mark your calendars to join the one that suits you best.

On-Demand Content
