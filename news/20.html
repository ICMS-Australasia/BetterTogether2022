A New Year, A New Outlook!

Happy New Year from the Better Together 2022 team! We hope you were able to take some time for yourself and spend some quality time with your family and friends. With the New Year we reflect on the recent struggles that we know people have faced and look towards what we hope is the light at the end of the pandemic tunnel.

The Conference Organising team is now back from holidays and in full planning mode for the final home stretch of the Conference lead-up. Although we will not be together in-person we can assure you that we are putting together an engaging and interactive program with some special touches not to be missed. This Conference aims to bring together clinicians, researchers, people with disability, families, carers, support workers and many others to share knowledge about childhood onset disability.

A Sneak Peek

Take a look at the first preview of the conference platform, get an insight into some special sessions and start to get excited about what is coming up at Better Together 2022.

Ask Me Anything Sessions

These are interactive sessions which provides the perfect opportunity to meet the keynotes or participate in a topic specific networking session. These sessions will be great for early career researchers and anyone looking to further discuss information from topics discussed throughout the program.

Feel Good Sessions

Feel good sessions are aimed to break up your day from the scientific program and put a smile on your dial.

These sessions include Comedy by Oliver Hunter who brings humour and insight to his stories and experiences of living with cerebral palsy, an inspiring dance performance choreographed by one of Emotion 21’s artists Jamie Haliday and an interview with Paralympic gold medal winner Paige Greco.
